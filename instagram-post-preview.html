💡 Instagram Tips

📐

4:5 Portrait Wins

The 4:5 ratio takes up the most vertical feed space. 1080×1350px is the highest-impact format for engagement.

🪝

Hook in Line 1

Only ~125 chars show before "more." Put your strongest statement or question in the very first line.

#️⃣

5–15 Hashtags

Meta recommends 5–15 relevant hashtags. Niche tags outperform mega-popular ones for actual reach.

🎬

Reels Get Priority

Instagram's algorithm promotes Reels 3–5× more than static posts. Convert top content to Reel format.

❓ FAQs

For feed posts, 1080×1350px (4:5 portrait) occupies the most vertical space and performs best. Square (1080×1080px) is the safest universal format. Landscape (1080×566px) takes up the least feed space. For Reels, always use 1080×1920px (9:16) for full-screen viewing.
Meta's guidance (2024) recommends 5–15 highly relevant hashtags per post. Using the first comment for hashtags keeps captions clean while maintaining discoverability. Mix large, medium, and niche hashtags for the best balance of reach and ranking chance.
