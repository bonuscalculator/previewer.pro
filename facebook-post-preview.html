📊 Post Analysis

💡 Facebook Tips

🖼️

Optimal Image

Use 1200×630px for photo posts. Square 1200×1200 works best on mobile feed presence.

✂️

Hook Early

Only ~125 chars show before "See More." Lead with your value prop in the first line.

🏷️

1–3 Hashtags

Facebook recommends 1–3 targeted hashtags. More than 5 actively reduces organic reach.

📅

Best Times

Tue–Thu 9–11am and 1–3pm local time show highest engagement across most industries.

❓ FAQs

Facebook posts support up to 63,206 characters. However, only ~125–400 characters display before the "See More" link. Write your hook in the first 2–3 lines so it's visible without clicking.
Images typically generate 2–3× more engagement than text-only posts. Native video gets even higher reach. High-quality text posts from genuine personal profiles can outperform low-quality image posts from Pages.
