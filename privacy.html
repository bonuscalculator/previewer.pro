Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

At Previewer.pro, we approach privacy with a very simple framework: we do not want, collect, or store your content. Because our tool processes your data entirely within your browser window, your input text and asset links remain strictly yours.

1. Information We Do Not Collect (Your Content)

When you paste social media captions, URLs, tags, or text into our preview apps, that data is processed locally inside your web browser. It is never uploaded to our servers, never saved in a database, and never shared with external APIs or third-party social media platforms.

2. Information We Do Collect

To keep the platform optimized, performant, and safe, we collect minimal operational information:

  • Log Data: Standard server logs containing your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and timestamp.
  • Anonymized Analytics: We may utilize basic, privacy-respecting analytics engines to monitor aggregate site traffic, general geographic locations, and feature engagement metrics (e.g., tracking which preview tabs are clicked most frequently).

3. Third-Party Links & API Scripts

Previewer.pro displays visual approximations of how posts render across third-party networks (such as Facebook, X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Threads). We do not load platform-specific tracking SDKs for these mockups. However, if you click external reference links or resource guides on our site, you will be subject to those platforms' respective privacy protocols.

4. Data Retention

Since your generated mockups exist entirely within ephemeral browser memory, your session content disappears the second you close or refresh your browser tab.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally refine this Privacy Policy to reflect security enhancements or tool architecture upgrades. Any modifications will be posted openly on this page with an updated timestamp.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our operational structure or local browser-rendering process, please contact us at privacy@previewer.pro.
