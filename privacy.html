The short version: We don't store the URLs you enter, we don't sell your data, we don't require an account, and we use minimal analytics only to improve our tools. Your privacy is a core design principle at Previewer.pro — not an afterthought.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Previewer.pro ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and online tools at previewer.pro (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all tools on the Previewer.pro platform, including our Mobile Viewer, SERP Preview Tool, Social Media Preview tools, and all related web pages.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit Previewer.pro, our servers and analytics services may automatically log certain technical information:

  • Log data: IP address (anonymised), browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps. This data is retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.
  • Device information: Screen resolution, browser language, and device type (desktop/mobile/tablet) — used only to improve tool responsiveness.
  • Usage patterns: Which tools are used, feature interactions, and session duration — used in aggregate, anonymised form to understand which features are most useful.

2.2 Information You Provide

If you contact us via our contact form or email, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (required to respond)
  • The content of your message
  • Any optional information you choose to include

Contact form data is retained only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and is deleted within 12 months unless an ongoing support relationship is established.

3. What We Don't Collect

This is what we believe matters most to you:

  • URLs you enter into our tools — URLs you paste into the Mobile Viewer, SERP Preview, or Social Media Preview tools are processed client-side in your browser and are NOT transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Page content — We do not store, index, or retain any content from URLs you preview.
  • Personal identification — We do not require registration, accounts, or login. We don't collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment information through normal tool usage.
  • Sensitive data — We never intentionally collect health data, financial data, or sensitive personal categories.

4. How We Use Data

The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Previewer.pro service
  • Understand which tools and features are most useful (aggregate analytics only)
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to security threats and abuse
  • Respond to support inquiries and contact form submissions
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or behavioural tracking. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking

5.1 Essential Cookies

We use only minimal, essential cookies required for the service to function correctly — such as session management and preference storage (e.g., remembering your selected device frame). These cookies are session-based and expire when you close your browser.

5.2 Analytics

We use privacy-respecting analytics (configured with IP anonymisation and no cross-site tracking) to understand aggregate usage patterns. These analytics do not use third-party advertising cookies or build individual profiles.

5.3 No Third-Party Advertising Cookies

We do not use advertising networks, retargeting pixels, Facebook Pixels, or Google Ads remarketing cookies on Previewer.pro. Your browsing on our site does not contribute to advertising profiles on other platforms.

⚠️ Browser preferences: You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling all cookies may affect essential tool functionality such as preference persistence.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Previewer.pro:

  • Hosting infrastructure: Our website is hosted on cloud infrastructure with servers located in the European Union and United States, compliant with data transfer regulations.
  • Google Fonts: We load web fonts from Google Fonts. This involves your browser making requests to Google's servers. Review Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Analytics: We use privacy-focused, GDPR-compliant web analytics with data anonymisation enabled.

We do not share personal data with third parties except as described above, or where required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes.
  • CCPA rights (California residents): The right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@previewer.pro. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Strict access controls limiting internal data access to authorised personnel only
  • Automatic log purging after defined retention periods

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we implement strong safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use secure, unique passwords and keep your browser up to date.

9. Children's Privacy

Previewer.pro is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact privacy@previewer.pro and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

  • Email: privacy@previewer.pro
