// faq

Questions, answered plainly.

Is Previewer.pro free to use?+

Yes. Every preview on Previewer.pro is free, with no account, sign-up, or credit card required.

Which platforms can I preview my post for?+

Previewer.pro supports Facebook, X (formerly Twitter), LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Threads, with more platforms in development.

Does Previewer.pro post anything on my behalf?+

No. Previewer.pro only renders a visual preview in your browser. It never connects to your accounts and never publishes anything for you.

Do I need to install anything?+

No installation is needed. Previewer.pro runs entirely in your browser at previewer.pro.

Does Previewer.pro store my captions?+

No. Captions are rendered locally in your browser for preview purposes and are not uploaded or stored on a server.
