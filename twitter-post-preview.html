💡 Twitter / X Tips

✂️

Hook First 2 Lines

X shows the first 2–3 lines before "Show more." Put your hook, question, or bold claim at the very start.

🧵

Threads Get More Reach

Threads consistently outperform single tweets in impressions. The algo rewards content that keeps users on-platform longer.

#️⃣

1–2 Hashtags Max

Unlike Instagram, hashtag stuffing hurts reach on X. Use 1–2 highly relevant hashtags or none at all.

📊

Polls Drive Engagement

Polls are one of the highest-engagement post formats on X. They force binary choices that drive comments and algorithm signals.

❓ FAQs

Standard accounts have a 280-character limit. X Premium subscribers get up to 4,000 characters (basic) or 25,000 characters (Premium+). URLs always count as 23 characters regardless of their actual length.
For single image tweets, 1200×675px (16:9) renders optimally on all devices. Square (1200×1200px) works universally. Maximum file size is 5MB for images. X crops images in feed to approximately 2:1 ratio.
