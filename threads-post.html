// writing tips

Posts that get engagement.

01

Hook in the first line

The first line is what shows in the feed preview. Put the most compelling statement right at the start.

02

One clear idea per post

Threads works best for focused, single-idea posts. Use the preview to check that your message isn't getting lost.

03

Check image readability

If you're posting text on an image, make sure it's readable in both light and dark modes. Use the toggle to verify.

04

Keep links clean

The link preview shows title and image. Make sure both match your message before you post.

// faq

Questions, answered plainly.

What is the character limit for a Threads post?+

Threads posts are limited to 500 characters. This tool counts characters live and warns you when you go over the limit.

Can I preview an image with my Threads post?+

Yes. Upload an image and it will appear inside the mockup exactly where it would in a real Threads post, so you can check the crop and composition.

Can I preview a link on Threads?+

Yes. Toggle to link mode and fill in the URL, title, and upload an image to see how the link preview will render in the feed.

Does this tool post to Threads for me?+

No. This is a preview-only tool. It never connects to your Threads account and never publishes anything on your behalf.

Can I see how my post looks in dark mode?+

Yes. Use the light/dark toggle above the preview to check how your post renders in both of Threads' display modes.

Is my post text or image stored anywhere?+

Your draft text is saved only in your own browser's local storage so it survives a refresh. Uploaded images are not saved and nothing is sent to a server.
